About Nyaki Adult Family Home

We are a trusted adult family home in Auburn, providing compassionate, person-centered care across two dedicated locations. We create a safe and supportive environment where residents feel at home and families feel confident in their care.

Our Vision

To create a trusted home environment where high-quality care, respect, and meaningful connection define the standard of adult family living.

Our Philosophy

Nyaki stands for thoughtful, compassionate care that upholds dignity, independence, and a true sense of home.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, individualized care that enhances the well-being, comfort, dignity, and independence of every resident.

What we Provide

Licensed adult family home care

24/7 personalized support

Individualized care plans

Safe, structured living environment

Consistent communication with families

Our Core Values

Compassion in every aspect of care

Dignity and respect for every resident

Excellence in the quality of care we provide

Integrity in all our actions and decisions

A strong sense of community for everyone
